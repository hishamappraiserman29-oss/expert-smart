# Skill: Advisory Narrative & Explanation Boxes

## Purpose
Enhance the end-user experience by translating dry numerical results and complex International Valuation Standards (IVS) metrics into simple, reassuring, and consultative language.

## Context
- **Target Output**: Valuation reports, Word document generation, or AI responses given to the end user.
- **Audience**: Ordinary readers, property owners, or non-technical bank staff.

## Execution Rules
1. **Consultative Explanation (صناديق التفسير)**: After presenting numerical data (like LTV ratios, ASR indexes, or DCF NPVs), always append an advisory paragraph or "Explanation Box".
2. **Simple Language**: Use plain, professional Arabic. Avoid overly dense financial jargon in the explanation box. Explain *what the number means* for the client's decision-making process.
3. **Reassurance**: Frame the findings in a way that builds trust, indicating that the system's calculations adhere to stringent global standards while keeping the client's risk profile in mind.
